GiggleLand is a 3D adventure game for Steam.

Embrace your inner child and explore the theme park of your wildest dreams. Meet fun characters, travel on unique rides, and play creative games on your journey from the entrance to the castle for a big surprise!

There is no fighting, You will experience no pain.

You will feel loved.

You will giggle and smile forever.

Meet our Characters!

Tolie is the main character of our game. She’s a quiet little girl exploring the park. She hasn’t had a very happy life…but we promise GiggleLand will make her smile! She’s a bit shy and can get lost pretty easily, so make sure you help her stay on the right track!

This adorable plushie is based off of Rory the Rabbit from the hit cartoon “Terry’s Vegetable Patch.” Rory may be a jokester, but he’s always there for his friends! He can help guide those who are lost and will always be there to talk to you.

This dynamic duo is as sweet as can be! Eliot is a warm and friendly cat who welcomes everyone with open arms. His best friend, Ella, always knows how to keep things lively and exciting for all. If you see these two in the park, make sure you say hello!
